about us

Orich Trading & Projects is a Gauteng based construction company established in 2021 with two very capable and hands on managing directors. The company’s mission is to establish a one-stop shop for any quality, small-scale construction services and solutions that will serve the local clients according to their budgets and business objectives, and ultimately continue to growth the company provincially & nationally.

mission statement

To develop, facilitate and provide reliable products & quality service.

Vision

  • To provide quality, affordable service.
  • To promote teamwork to empower quality work.
  • To promote good values that will increase the credibility of the company.
  • A team based approach that guarantees constant and superior results.
  • To serve the country by promoting self-reliance through job creation.
  • To promote a work environment that ensures no discrimination in any form.
  • To continue to be in alignment with the country’s economic goal
