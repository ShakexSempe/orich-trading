about us

Orich Trading & Projects is a construction company located in Johannesburg, Gauteng that was established in 2021 and currently has one director and one supporting role employee. The company’s mission is to establish a one-stop shop for any quality, small-scale construction services and solutions that will serve the local clients according to their budgets and business objectives, and ultimately continue to growth the company provincially and nationally.

mission statement

To develop, facilitate and provide responsible quality service to the community

Vision

  • To provide quality, affordable service.
  • To promote teamwork to empower quality work.
  • To promote good values that will increase the credibility of the company.
  • A team based approach that guarantees constant and superior results.
  • To serve the country by promoting self-reliance through job creation.
